Why?

W3C creates, describes and recommends standards for CSS. Browsers do not follow these standards, and standards are not too sharpened to convenient use. OSWS project describes the tools to achieve cross-browser code without having to know the subtleties of different browsers, convenience, creating flexible and comfortable styles without templates and predefined classes.

What?

Stylus in contrast to LESSCSS allows you to create mixins and use them as a substitute with the same options as in the standard W3C syntax, and with greatly expanded and simplified.
